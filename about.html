About Us

Bright Kids E-Learning is a global education initiative and a non-profit organization providing free, fun, curriculum based quality primary education to all children world wide. The foundation was launched in Malaysia in 2020. Our goal is to help children build a strong foundation for future academic success by providing a comprehensive and engaging online curriculum to greatly assist early learners to succeed in pre-k, kindergarten, and early elementary school programs. Our vision is to be the source for childhood learning on the internet available from anywhere and without charge. Bright Kids E-Learning is subscription-based, with no advertising, pop-up ads, or links to other sites. Children can learn and explore with their parents, or on their own (depending on age and ability), in a safe and secure online educational environment. We named our site Bright Kids E-Learning because here, children can learn important educational basics as bride kids with bride futures. We believe that the word "Bright" can attract kids around the world and also the parents and give them the idea of that this E-Learning website provides thousands of benefits for both parents and children.
